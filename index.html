Remote Controlled Car


In this project, I got the experience to etch and solder electronic components on printed circuits boards. I drilled onto the etched PCB where I used a template circuit design for this project. I also programmed the car using PIC chips in MPLAB assembly. A simple two-wheel motor engine was used for this robot car. This was indeed a fun project!

Technologies used: Soldering iron, MPLAB assembly, PIC, LEDs and other various electronic components
